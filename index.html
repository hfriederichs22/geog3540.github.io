Geovisualization Portfolio GEOG 3540

The Average Population of Every Ohio District

A visualization project in which I created a choropleth map to show the average populations of every district in Ohio. The goal for this assignment was to use appropraite map colors to shows an easy to read and understandable visualization.

Populations of the worlds most urban areas

For this project I created a point symbol map showing the population in the largest urban areas in the world.

Percentage of Asian American's in every district in Los Angeles County

I created a bivariate map in Observable that shows the percentage of Asian Americans living in every district in LA county. The goal for creating this map was to show economic status in relation to geo-graphic location. Living in LA county is expensive as it is, but living in a coastal district within LA county, is a different story.

Presentation on Predicting CAD in Patients

This is a presentation that I did during my data management and visualizations class. For this project I used Tableau that was connected to a SQL database, to show how certain health variables can show signs of on-set or already existing coronary artery disease (CAD).
