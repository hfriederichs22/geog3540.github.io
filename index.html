CAD Presentation

A presentation that I did using SQL and Tableau to predict the possibilty of having CAD. Factors such as chest pain types, blood pressure, resting heart rate, as well as many more were taken into consideration when calling on data.

Final Project GEOG-3540

My final project for GEOG-3540 that shows world and US earthquakes. Data was taken from the USGS Earthquake Catalog and was used to map earthquakes happening from the years 2000-2014, as well as earthquakes that have happened within the past 30 days (April 2021-May 2021).
